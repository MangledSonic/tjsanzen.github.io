tjsanzen studios started

Mine-Imator

Our founder, Tovin Sannes-Venhuizen stared messing with a a program he found on the internet for free, called Mine-Imator. He started making a few animations, then still renders. After a few months of using Mine-Imator, he found Blender 3D. A free open source program.

Blender

After discovering Blender, Tovin tryed learning how to use it, but like all 3D softwares, there is a bit of a learning curve. After about 4 months of having the software, he finaly started to get results. Then he discovered the Game Engine.

Games with Blender

The built in Game Engine allowed people to create games right inside of Blender, with or without coding. Tovin quiqly learned the basics and started making games, but they all looked like trash. Then he discovered the differece between MutiTexture and GLSL shading. Oh, what a differece that made!
